Ouranoco is a small studio building iOS apps with a specialism in tools for Flight and Cabin crew. We care about the quiet details that make apps a pleasure to use.

i.
About us

A small team, a specific point of view.

Ouranoco is a private limited company registered in England and Wales. The name comes from Ouranos — the Greek personification of the sky — reflecting our roots in aviation and our enduring interest in the things that fly above it.

Several of our apps are built for Pilots and Cabin Crew, an audience whose working lives depend on information being reliable, clear, and instantly accessible. That discipline shapes everything we make — including the apps we build for everyone else.

We release regularly. We listen closely to the people who use our apps every day. And we believe good software should be liberating not frustrating.

ii.
How we work

Three ideas we keep returning to.

one

Native by default

Our apps are built for iOS with Swift and SwiftUI — not wrapped from the web, not ported from elsewhere. Platform conventions are features, not friction.

two

Built with users, not for them

Our apps are shaped by those who use them every day. Feedback becomes features quickly — we don't get bogged down in bureaucracy.

three

Quiet design

We believe the best software gets out of your way. Clear typography, considered spacing, and restraint over decoration. Always.
